Pillars of Persuasion
🏛️

CREDIBILITY BUILDER

Persuade through expertise, authority, and trustworthiness. Reference domain-specific language, certifications, and results. Make them believe you understand their world before they hear anything else.

❤️

EMOTION TRIGGER

Connect to their frustrations, fears, and aspirations. The decision-maker who feels understood is far more likely to engage. Pain-first language that mirrors their daily reality.

📊

LOGIC FOCUSED

Lead with specific numbers, clear cause-effect chains, and falsifiable claims. Give them intellectual permission to say yes. One precise, credible statistic outperforms five vague claims.

🧲
Principles of Influence

① Reciprocity

Share a useful insight or resource before asking for anything. Give first, always. The instinct to return favours is one of the most reliable forces in human behaviour.

② Commitment

Get micro-yeses: a connection accepted, a post liked, a question answered. Each small agreement builds momentum toward a larger one.

③ Social Proof

Reference similar companies or roles who have benefited. Even anonymised examples ("a manufacturer like yours") signal that others have trusted you.

④ Authority

Establish domain-specific expertise early. Name the exact frameworks, regulations, or challenges their industry cares about.

⑤ Liking

Mirror their language and acknowledge real pressures. Be warm, genuinely curious, and human. People buy from people they like.

⑥ Scarcity

Frame the conversation as mutual evaluation — you are choosing each other. Selectivity signals confidence and quality.

📚
Core Sales Frameworks

The Challenger Sale

Teach something new about their problem. Tailor the insight to their specific context. Take control by reframing their thinking before positioning your solution.

SPIN Selling

Situation → Problem → Implication → Need-Payoff. Ask what their current process looks like, then what happens when it breaks, then what that costs. Let them sell themselves.

Touchpoint Principles

Be genuinely interested in their journey. Remember names and details across every touchpoint. Talk in terms of their challenges — not your features.

Generosity Network

Build before you need. Share useful content, make warm introductions, comment meaningfully. Create a world where they want to take your call.

Hook–Story–Offer

Hook: a sharp, specific pain statement. Story: a result a similar company got. Offer: a low-commitment next step. Three sentences. Done.

Tactical Empathy

Label their feelings: "It sounds like you're under pressure to…" Slow, calm, certain tone. "No" is a starting point, not an ending. Mirror and let silence work.

AI API Configuration

Connect Sensells to AI. Use Proxy mode to keep your API key hidden from users (subscription model), or Direct mode for personal / developer use.

Connection Mode

In Proxy mode, all requests route through your backend server — users never see your AI API key. In Direct mode, each user provides their own key.

ℹ️Deploy server.js on Railway, Render, or any VPS. Set ANTHROPIC_API_KEY and SUBSCRIPTION_KEYS as environment variables.
ℹ️Your key is stored only in this browser's local storage. It is never sent to any server other than the API directly. See your AI provider console to create a key.

Model & Usage
